What to do in Penang

In case you have some time to explore more of what this island has to offer, here’s a 5-step guide on what to do in Penang! This covers eating the best of Penang’s Hawker food and musing over its best heritage spots and scenic environment.

Visit the Chowrasta Bazaar for fresh produce, local delicacies, and fun souvenirs.
Walk to Masjid Kapitan Keling, which is Georgetown’s largest historic mosque.
Explore the historic Khoo Kngsi complex, which consists of an administrative building with a meeting hall and offices, an opera stage, as well as 62 units of terrace houses and shop houses.
4. Shop at the 57th floor of KOMTAR, the tallest building in Georgetown. A stretch of duty free shops are located here. You can also go all the way up to its 65th floor for a panoramic view of Penang.
5. Eat at Jalan Nagore, where you can treat yourself to a Malay, Chinese, and Indian cuisines.
